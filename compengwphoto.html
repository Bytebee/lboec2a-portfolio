What is a Computer Engineer?

A computer engineer design, test, implement and maintain computer software and hardware systems. They ensure both the physical hardware components and software programming components work together smoothly for their clients. Some computer engineers specialize in fields like robotics, virtual reality, artificial intelligence, mobile apps and operating systems.


Computer Engineers:
  • build computers and computer systems
  • research software and computer hardware
  • design and test computer hardware components
  • manage operating systems
  • troubleshoot any concerns with hardware or software

Steps to become a Computer Engineer

  1. Obtain a bachelor's degree and consider earning a master's degree or a Ph.D. if you want to do research or be in a specialized field.
  2. Gain work experience.
  3. Earn certifications in hardware, software, or security to demonstrate your expertise.
  4. Create a resume showing your education, skills, and job experience.
